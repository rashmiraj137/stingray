***************
Citing Stingray
***************

If you use Stingray in your data analysis, **please cite us**!
Citations are still the main currency of the academic world, and pointing to citations is *the* best way to help us ensure that Stingray continues to be supported and we can continue working on it!

Stingray now has an official citation! When using Stingray, pleace cite the `ApJ paper <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2019ApJ...881...39H/abstract>`_ and the `JOSS paper<https://joss.theoj.org/papers/10.21105/joss.01393#>`. Here are the BibTeX entries for your convenience::
